Alternative Media, Alternative Narratives

How can the left craft its own story in a media landscape dominated by Billionaire Media Barons? It will explore how to build our own media institutions which we can use push for a true consensus around socialist ideas. It will also look at how we create our own narratives within a mainstream media that is systemically stacked against us by playing them at their own game.

Chair: Jasmine Ketibuah-Foley (Ujima Radio). Panel: Dawn Foster, Matt Turner (Evolve Politics), Steve Howell (Comms advisor to Corbyn GE2017 and Aurhor) and Alon Aviram (The Bristol Cable)

The Environment: Sustainable futures and a Green New Deal

How do we approach the drastic changes that are required to mitigate climate change and the destruction of the natural planet in the short time span left to us? How do we do this without putting the onus on individuals or the global south whilst corporations go on unfettered? How do we create structural changes that allow participatory, sustainable futures which improve the wellbeing of everyone and can be embraced by trade unions?
Chair: Rob Wotherspoon, Panel: Chris Saltmarsh, James Meadway, Kerry McCarthy MP, Skeena Rathor, Sakina Sheikh

Education

There is currently a 8% real terms cut in school funding and cuts to FE, and expected cuts to universities this year. With reduced local accountability, and years of cuts to pay and conditions for staff, this panel will be discussing these issues, how we can fightback in Bristol & South Glos, and campaigns that have worked elsewhere.

Chair: George Angus, Panel: Dani Wijesinghe (Early Years Practitioner), Leo Nikolaidis (Bristol NEU teacher), Nina Franklin, Ollie Turnbull and Amelia Horgan.
